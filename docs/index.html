Fortune recognition kit


Build your own projects to see good and bad luck in everything around you. The BIY See Boards comes preloaded with special trained models and functions!

  • BAD LUCK ALERT
    The board uses the camera feed to interpret the scene and inform the user of very unlucky configurations (e.g. a black cat crossing the street - 17 people sitting at a dinner table, or a person opening an umbrella indoor).
  • DEEPDREAM TO SMORFIA
    The camera interprets what it sees according to the “smorfia” a book that details the old Neapolitan tradition in which dreams are analyzed and converted into numbers.
  • FACE 2 ‘JETTATORE’
    Our Facial recognition is trained on specific facial physiognomies of people that normally bring bad luck (known as Jettatore)
